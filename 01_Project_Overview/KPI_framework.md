# KPI Framework

## Overview

This project adopts a Balanced Business Intelligence approach to monitor the performance of an e-learning marketplace across multiple strategic dimensions. The KPIs are designed to align with business objectives and provide actionable insights for decision-makers.

The framework is structured into four key pillars: Revenue, Instructor Performance, Course Portfolio, and Student Engagement.

---

## 1. Revenue and Financial Performance

These KPIs measure the overall business health and growth of the platform.

### Key Metrics

* Total Revenue
* Monthly Revenue Trend
* Revenue Growth Rate
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Revenue Contribution by Category
* Revenue Contribution by Instructor
* Impact of Discounting on Revenue
* Refund Rate and Payment Success Rate

### Business Value

These metrics help evaluate financial sustainability, pricing strategy, and category-level profitability.

---

## 2. Instructor Performance and Retention

Instructors are core value creators in the platform. Monitoring their productivity and engagement is essential.

### Key Metrics

* Revenue per Instructor
* Top vs Long-Tail Instructor Contribution
* Course Creation Frequency
* Instructor Activity Trend
* Instructor Churn and Retention Indicators
* Average Course Rating by Instructor
* Student Reach per Instructor

### Business Value

Supports identifying high-performing instructors, retention strategies, and operational load balancing.

---

## 3. Course Portfolio Optimization

This pillar focuses on product strategy and content effectiveness.

### Key Metrics

* Enrollment per Course
* Course Revenue Ranking
* Course Engagement and Popularity
* Category-Level Demand Analysis
* Underperforming Course Identification
* Course Lifecycle and Trend Analysis
* Domain Growth Tracking

### Business Value

Enables portfolio rationalization, content improvement, and strategic expansion.

---

## 4. Student Engagement and Behavior

Understanding student behavior is critical for long-term growth and retention.

### Key Metrics

* Active Students Over Time
* Enrollment by Hour and Day
* New vs Returning Students
* Category Preference Segmentation
* Seasonal and Behavioral Trends
* Platform Traffic Patterns

### Business Value

Improves user experience, marketing strategy, and platform design.

---

## KPI Selection Approach

Not all metrics are displayed in dashboards. KPIs are selected based on:

* Business relevance and decision-making impact.
* Clarity and interpretability.
* Ability to drive actionable insights.
* Avoidance of redundant or low-value metrics.

The framework ensures a balance between strategic and operational analytics, making the system scalable and adaptable to real-world business needs.

---

## Future Extensions

The KPI framework can be extended to include:

* Customer lifetime value (CLV)
* Subscription analytics
* Marketing and acquisition performance
* Personalized learning and recommendation metrics.

This structured approach provides a strong analytical foundation for continuous improvement and data-driven decision-making.
